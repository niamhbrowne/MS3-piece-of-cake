Getting to know us...

We are a self-taught cake/chocolate loving husband and wife team who run a small cake shop in Omagh, Co Tyrone. We pride ourselves as being one of the very few scratch bakeries in NI.

Before I set up shop I baked from home since 2018 selling cakes, cupcakes and brownies, on the side of full time jobs. I really wasn’t familiar with the term ‘scratch baking’ until I set up shop, where I had my eyes opened! 😱

I had lots of suppliers contact me wanting to sell me massive bags of cake, brownie, cookie etc ‘premix’ as that’s what all the bakeries use they said. I was so so against it and still am! There’s just no way it would taste the same as homemade!

Quality

So in our kitchen we use high end ingredients such as callebaut chocolate, (which ain’t cheap! 😂) Real branded confectionary and spreads, no fake Maltesers, Mars bars or Nutella lol I didn’t even know this was a thing!! And of course the best free range eggs from Ezi Eggs 😍😍

It’s what I’ve always done and what I’ve always known, I would never compromise on taste or quality, being a chocoholic myself I can sniff out cheap waxy chocolate a mile off 😂

This of course is reflected in our prices. At present there’s no one else like us in our small town (I mean shops) and I know there are lots of people who might suck air through their teeth when they see a brownie costs £2.20 - but that’s the cost of quality, hand made, scratch baked goods 🙌🏻🙌🏻

Thank You!

We are very much small scale, and try to replicate that home baked taste! Also I can’t understand how it’s ok for chain coffee shops to sell a factory produced frozen brownie for more than ours, but I guess that’s just how the whole industry works🤷🏼‍♀️🙈

It’s so easy to get overwhelmed but I’ll be making a real effort to get back into the kitchen again, I’ve gradually been doing less and less baking with having all the other businessey stuff to do and I hate it lol so I can’t wait to get back and come up with new fresh ideas!

So THANK YOU to everyone who gets what we’re about and who has supported our dream since we opened last year. I never thought we would have ‘regular’ customers, it’s just amazing 💖💖💖
